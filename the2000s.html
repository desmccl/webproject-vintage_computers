The early 2000s saw the introduction of the famous iMac G3. Coming in 5 colors with macthing notebooks they were extremely popular. It is no wonder Apple seems to be heading back in that direction with the current release of their latest M1 iMacs.

Image courtesy of Mac Users Guide

The matching notebooks were also very popular despite the introductory price tag at $1,599. Specs were a 300MHz PowerPC processor and 288-320 MB of RAM!

Image courtesy of Mac Users Guide

The iMac G5 introduced the look and feel that is most familiar with users today.
